Environment

Caring for the Environment is as important as caring for our Communities.

Our shop Aim and Goals are to be one of the most Ethical and Environmentally friendly shop in the UK. We are using the latest environmentally friendly methods to improve our service and contribution towards the environment.At the same time we are ensuring we offer the best products brought to us from fair-trade suppliers

Re-usable Cups

In our store we have introduced the new Re-cyclable cups. They will help our willing-to-recycle customers to help the environment by re-using these cups and helping the environment to reduce the plastic on the planet. The cups will cost £0.50 to our customers and every time they use it in our store they will get the same amount discounted from any of the coffees we offer.

Preserving Water

Water is a precious natural resource and an important ingredient in our business. That’s why we are looking for new ways to be responsible with the water we use, consume and sell. . We are using the latest waterless machines to ensure we are not wasting water and at the same time we are contributing to the environment. In the future we are planning to use solar panels for our electricity and for the heating systems.
