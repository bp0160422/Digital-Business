About Us

We are an independent coffee shop located at Shepherd’s Bush market. We aim to be the most ethical and ecological coffee company in the UK, by using the most ecological and environmental friendly methods to run our business.Open in 2019 we have been serving our customers coffee and freshly made food. Here you will discover the more about our products, prices, our responsibilities as a business and the contribution to the environment.

MAKE WHAT YOU LOVE WHAT YOU MAKE

We are an independent business, passionate about serving you the very best organic, Fairtrade coffee, and proper, handmade food. Here you’ll discover more about our seasonal, freshly prepared menu, drum roasted coffee, as well as a bit about our people and all the scoop from OZO COFFE.

We want our employees and customers to be satisfied. When it comes the food, we are not into mass production. We want to make sure that everything we do is top quality even if that mean that only a limited amount per day of sandwiches will be made. That is one thing that differentiate us from others.
